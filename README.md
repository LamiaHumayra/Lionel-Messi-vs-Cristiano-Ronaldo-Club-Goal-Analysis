# Lionel-Messi-vs-Cristiano-Ronaldo-Club-Goal-Analysis

I'm a huge football fan, and with the FIFA hype everywhere right now, I finally had the perfect excuse to dive back into SQL.

I analyzed the complete **club goal history** of Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo, and I almost forgot how much I genuinely enjoy writing SQL queries. There's something really satisfying about turning thousands of rows into stories.

A few things that stood out:

⚽ Messi scored an incredible **474 LaLiga goals**, while Ronaldo finished with **311**.
🎯 Sevilla FC might not enjoy looking at this dataset... they're the club both players loved scoring against the most.
🦶 No surprises here—Messi's left foot is simply ridiculous (**436 left-footed goals**), while Ronaldo's goals are spread much more evenly across both feet, headers, and penalties.
🤝 Luis Suárez assisted Messi **48** times, while Karim Benzema tops Ronaldo's list with **44** assists.
📍 Ronaldo scored most of his club goals as a **left winger**, while Messi's goals are almost perfectly split between **centre forward** and **right wing**.

Of course, no data project is complete without messy data. 😄

The dataset had a few encoding issues—competitions like *Trophée des Champions* appeared under multiple spellings because of character encoding, and some playing positions had extra spaces, creating duplicate categories. A little data cleaning was needed before the analysis could begin.

**Tools:** MySQL Workbench 8.0 (SQL)
**Dataset:** Kaggle (Club Goal History of Lionel Messi & Cristiano Ronaldo)

It's always fun when football and data analytics come together. ⚽📊
